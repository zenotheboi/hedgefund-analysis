# Hedge Fund Analysis: Clinical Trial Outcome Prediction

## Purpose

The primary objective of this repository is to investigate the feasibility of building a successful hedge fund centered around a predictive model for clinical trial outcomes. 

**Important Scope Note:** This analysis strictly focuses on therapeutics clinical trials for **small molecules and/or small peptides**. No other modalities or trial types are considered in this investigation.

## Methodology & Project Phases

This investigation is structured into the following sequential steps:

### Step 1: Price Movement Archetype Analysis
Analyze historical price movement archetypes of biopharmaceutical stocks resulting from the announcement of clinical trial outcomes. This includes analyzing the impact of both positive and negative results across Phase 1, Phase 2, and Phase 3 trials. 

### Step 2: Perfect Information Backtesting (100% Accuracy)
Backtest various trading strategies assuming our predictive model is 100% accurate. Given the historical ~90% failure rate in clinical trials and the corresponding market anticipation of failure, we will evaluate strategies such as:
*   Long positions
*   Short selling
*   Long positions + renting out shares to short sellers

### Step 3: Realistic Accuracy Backtesting (90% Accuracy)
Identify the optimal strategy derived from Step 2 and backtest it assuming a high, yet imperfect, model accuracy of 90%.

### Step 4: True Model Predictions Backtesting
Perform a final backtest of the optimal strategy using the actual, empirical predictions generated by our true predictive model.
